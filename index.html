Arbotix RoboController

The Arbotix robocontroller is an Arduino compatible microcontroller board, designed to control small-to-medium sized robots, especially walkers that use Bioloid AX-12 servos. Support is typically handled through the Trossen Robotics Community Forum. You can purchase an Arbotix from Trossen Robotics.

PyPose is a related project for an open-source pose capture program.
